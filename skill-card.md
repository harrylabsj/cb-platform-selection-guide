## Description: <br>
Choose the right ecommerce platform for any overseas market in minutes by comparing marketplaces, DTC, social commerce, and wholesale channels by margin, category fit, operational readiness, and policy risk. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External ecommerce founders, marketplace operators, DTC brand teams, and cross-border expansion managers use this skill to compare overseas marketplace, DTC, social commerce, distributor, reseller, wholesale, or hybrid channels and produce a structured launch planning framework with assumptions and validation tasks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Platform policies, fee schedules, and eligibility rules can change after the skill's examples were written. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current platform terms and launch requirements directly before making channel or budget decisions. <br>
Risk: Business-planning outputs could be mistaken for legal, tax, financial, or compliance advice. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as a planning aid and review local laws, tax obligations, compliance requirements, and launch commitments with reliable sources or qualified professionals. <br>
